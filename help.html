1. Getting Started

To use Nova Prime, sign up as either a freelancer or client. After registration, you can create projects or apply to jobs immediately.

2. How to Post a Job

Clients can post jobs by navigating to the dashboard and selecting "Post New Job". Fill in all relevant details and submit for review.

3. How to Apply for a Job

Freelancers can browse available jobs on the listings page. Click on a job to view details and submit your application or proposal directly.

4. Payment Questions

Nova Prime uses secure payment processors. Funds are held in escrow until the job is marked complete. Payments are usually released within 3 business days.
