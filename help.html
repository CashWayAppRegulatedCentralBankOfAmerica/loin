1. Getting Started with Nova Prime

How do I create a Nova Prime account?

Simply click the “Sign Up” button on the homepage and fill in your full name, email address, mobile number, and password. You'll be prompted to verify your email and complete your profile to start working or hiring.

Is Nova Prime free to use?

Yes, creating an account is completely free. Freelancers only pay a small service fee when earning, and clients only pay when they hire.

2. Freelancers

How do I find work?

After completing your profile, head to the "Find Work" section to browse open jobs. Submit bid and wait for an email from Nova Prime on how you will work on the task and produce your task after completion.

How do I get paid?

Once the client marks your task as completed and releases payment, we will send you an email with the Payment verification code, which you will head to "My Projects" click on verify, input the CODE and your payment will reflect as "Paid", your funds will reflect in your Nova Prime account Immediately. You can withdraw funds via PayPal, bank transfer, or other available methods depending on your Country.

Why is my payment Unpaid?

Payments may take up to 24–48 hours AFTER you submited your Task to process Unpaid. If your task is marked as Unpaid after 48 hours then you will receive an email on the reason towards that which includes: Rejected Work or Delays from the Client.

3. Clients

How do I hire a freelancer?

Sent a job to our email at client@1234 and it will be uploaded to our Platform for a Freelancer to bid, when a Freelancer bids you will receive an email Instantly, Once you find someone that fits, reply with "Hire". An automated email will be sent to the Freelancer Instantly with your Task ID for he or she to start working on your Task. You’ll only be charged once the freelancer completes the job.

Can I cancel a job?

Yes, you can cancel a job at any point before work begins. If the milestone is funded, it will be refunded to your payment method.

4. Security & Disputes

Is Nova Prime safe?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption and two-step verification to protect your account. We also monitor activity to prevent fraud and abuse.

What if there's a dispute?

If a freelancer or client has an issue with a contract, our dispute team can step in. Start by messaging the other party. If no resolution is reached, open a formal dispute through your dashboard.

5. Technical Questions

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it via email.

Why am I not receiving emails from Nova Prime?

Please check your spam or junk folder. Make sure to whitelist support@novaprime.com to receive all important messages.
