Nova Prime Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Nova Prime values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our freelance platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, location, and profile details.
  • Financial Details: Payment information such as banking details (for freelancers) or card details (for clients).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, access time, pages visited.
  • Communication: Messages between users, support queries, reviews.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Nova Prime platform.
  • Facilitate communication between clients and freelancers.
  • Process payments and withdrawals securely.
  • Detect and prevent fraud or illegal activity.
  • Improve platform performance and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:

  • With payment processors (e.g., PayPal, Stripe) to facilitate transactions.
  • With legal authorities if required by law.
  • With service providers under strict confidentiality to help operate our platform.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we store about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent or object to certain data uses.
  • Request data portability in a structured, machine-readable format.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Upon account closure, your data may be archived for legal and security reasons.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Nova Prime uses cookies and similar technologies to personalize content, analyze traffic, and enhance your experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Nova Prime is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Security

We implement security measures to protect your data, including encryption, firewalls, and access controls. While we strive to protect your information, no system is 100% secure.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the United States of America. Any disputes related to this policy shall fall under the jurisdiction of U.S. courts.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will post the revised policy on our platform and update the effective date.
